About Droptrens

Empowering gym owners with automation to scale their business.

Our Story

Droptrens was born out of frustration. Our founders, who were managing multiple gym branches across Delhi, found that they were spending more time chasing pending fees and managing Excel sheets than actually focusing on fitness and growth. Existing software solutions were either too complex, too expensive, or lacked basic features like automated WhatsApp reminders.

We built Droptrens to solve these exact problems. Today, it has evolved into a comprehensive suite that completely automates fee tracking, member management, and communication, allowing gym owners to focus on what they do best: building a healthier community.

Our Mission

To provide an intuitive, affordable, and powerful automation tool that completely eliminates the administrative headaches of running a gym in India.

Our Vision

To become the standard operating system for every fitness center across India, driving digital transformation in the fitness industry.
